# Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** July 11, 2026

WallBang (“we”, “us”) operates wallbang.xyz and related Counter-Strike 2 community services.

## Information we collect

When you join the waitlist, we collect your name and email address. If you join our Discord, Discord’s privacy policy applies to that platform. Future Steam login will process Steam identity data needed for authentication and player profiles.

## How we use information

We use waitlist data to communicate launch updates, server availability, and product news related to WallBang. We do not sell personal data.

## Cookies and analytics

The marketing site may use essential cookies required for security and basic functionality. If analytics are added later, this policy will be updated before collection begins.

## Data retention

Waitlist submissions are retained until you request deletion or the waitlist purpose is complete. Contact us via Discord to request deletion.

## Third parties

We may use infrastructure providers (hosting, email delivery) that process data on our behalf under contractual obligations.

## Children’s privacy

WallBang is intended for players who meet Steam and Counter-Strike 2 age requirements. We do not knowingly collect data from children below those requirements.

## Changes

We may update this policy as the platform grows (VIP, Steam login, statistics). Material changes will be posted on this page.

## Contact

Questions about privacy: join [Discord](https://discord.gg/zE2Xvhgyq5) and open a ticket with the moderation team.
